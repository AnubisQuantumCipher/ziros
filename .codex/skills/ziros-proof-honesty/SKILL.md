# ZirOS Proof Honesty

- Never relabel bounded, delegated, metadata-only, or advisory evidence as mechanized proof.
- Source release-grade proof claims from the verification ledger and related truth surfaces only.
- Fail closed on strict-lane downgrade.
- Keep `trusted_protocol_tcb`, `hypothesis-carried`, and `model-only` boundaries explicit.
